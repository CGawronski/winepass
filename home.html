What is WinePass?

WinePass gives you unlimited access to all of Niagara Region's finest wineries and vineyards. Enjoy private tours and wine-making lessons or relax with personalized wine tastings paired with seasonal dishes from the winery's kitchen.

WinePass will also take care of your accommodations and transportation to and from the wineries, making your trip a breeze. Just select the activities for each winery you wish to visit and we take care of the rest.
